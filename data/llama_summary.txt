LLaMA (Large Language Model Meta AI) is a family of large language models developed by Meta AI, starting with LLaMA 1 in February 2023 and the latest release being LLaMA 4 in April 2025. The models are available in different sizes, ranging from 1 billion to 2 trillion parameters, and are designed to improve performance through scaling laws. LLaMA 2, released in mid-2023, included fine-tuned models for chat and code. LLaMA 3 followed with multilingual and multimodal capabilities. LLaMA 4, launched in 2025, introduced a mixture of experts architecture, outperforming previous models like GPT-4o in certain benchmarks.
